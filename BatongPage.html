Ingredients:
  • 2 ampalaya
  • 3 table spoons of oil
  • 1 minsed garlic
  • 1 minsed onion
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of salt
  • 1 ginisa mix(optional)
  • 5 table spoons of sugar(optional)
Ginisang Batong

Procedure

  1. Place the ampalaya in a large bowl
  2. Add salt and lukewarm water then leave for 5 minutes
  3. Squeeze tightly the ampalaya until all liquid drips
  4. Heat the pan and place the cooking oil
  5. Saute the garlic, onion, and tomato
  6. Add the ampalaya mix well with the other ingredients
  7. Beat the eggs and pour over the ampalaya then let the eggs cook partially
  8. Mix the egg with the other ingredients
  9. Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!
